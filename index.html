Here is Michael Jordan's Career

  • 1984 - Drafted by Chicago Bulls on 3rd pick
  • 1985 - His first All Star
  • 1988 - Win his first MVP (he won many after that)
  • 1991 - His perfect year (NBA Championship, MVP, Scoring Title, Finals MVP)
  • 1993 - Won his first Three peat
  • 1996 - Won his 4th title after come back from his baseball career and get his second perfect year (or better with ASG MVP)
  • 1998 - Won his second threepeat and becoming arguably the best basketball player ever
